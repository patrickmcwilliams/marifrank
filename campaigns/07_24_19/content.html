WHY COMPETENCY & CONSCIOUSNESS THROUGH MINDFULNESS?
As lawyers, we often deal with high conflict, stressed out, demanding clients - and sometimes opposing counsel display these negative emotions too. Those reactions are contagious unless we have the skills to remain calm in the mist of the “storm”.  Mindfulness is a tool to keep us on an even keel, manage our own emotions, and increase our competency. The deliberate use of mindfulness techniques are proven to reduce stress, increase focus, improve emotion and physical well being, increase respect and empathy, and boost our emotional intelligence. This program will give you the tools you need to transform stress into effectiveness and greater competency.
